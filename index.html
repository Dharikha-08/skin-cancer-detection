DermVision

Advanced AI for Skin Cancer Detection

DermVision utilizes state-of-the-art multimodal Artificial Intelligence to detect potential skin malignancies with high precision. By fusing dermatoscopic image analysis with clinical patient metadata, we provide a comprehensive risk evaluation system for early support.

Medical Disclaimer: This is an AI-automated detection tool. Please be aware that AI can sometimes provide incorrect results. Always consult a qualified doctor or dermatologist for a professional diagnosis before confirming any medical results.

How It Works

Our multimodal process ensures clinical-grade precision.

1

Image Upload

Upload a high-resolution dermatoscopic scan of the skin lesion for visual feature extraction.

2

Metadata Entry

Provide essential clinical markers like location, sex, and elevation to ground the image analysis.

3

AI Fusion

Our EfficientNet-B2 and XGBoost models fuse data to calculate a precise malignancy risk score.

Why DermVision AI?

AI-driven diagnostics allow for rapid screening and early detection, which is crucial in effective skin cancer treatment. Our system leverages millions of trained clinical data points to identify patterns invisible to the human eye.
